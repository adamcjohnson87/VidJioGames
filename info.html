Game Information - Select a game series below to see information.

Mass Effect

Epic Sci-Fi Trilogy followed up with a stand alone game. The original trilogy was very popular in its time. The second game wasn't received as well as the original, but was still a solid game.

Final Fantasy

A never ending role playing series with dozens of entries. The numbered series goes up to 15 at the time of this writing. However, many of those numbered entries have branched off into other games, and quite a few stand alone entries.

Resident Evil

A survival horror series based around a shady organization known as Umbrella, zombies and odd mutants included. Players fight zombies and various other mutants with limited resources.

Elder Scrolls

A role playing epic that takes place in a fantasy world known as Nirn on the continent of Tamriel. There is currently a total of 5 main titles, with a few spin offs. The last title was released in 2011 and is still modded and played to this day.

God of War

A high octane action game that takes place in ancient greece. You play as Kratos who has been used and abused by the gods. In these stories, he seeks revenge on them.

Doom

A popular shooter series that has had a recent comeback since originally launching decades ago. You play as a super marine who's sole job is to kill demons from hell.

Diablo

An action role playing looting game. Choose your class and go up against the lords of hell, all while gaining precious randomized loot.

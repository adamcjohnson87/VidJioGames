Vid-Jio Reviews

Mass Effect

Epic Sci-Fi Trilogy followed up with a stand alone game. The original trilogy was very popular in its time. The first standalone game after the original trilogy wasn't received as well as the original, but was still a solid game. No matter what, whether your a shooter or RPG fan, this series can rarely disappoint.
VidJio Current Standing Score: 10/10

Final Fantasy

A never ending role playing series with dozens of entries. The numbered series goes up to 15 at the time of this writing. However, many of those numbered entries have branched off into other games, and quite a few stand alone entries. Not all final fantasy games are alike, and some are not as great as the others. So tough to score the entire series.
VidJio Current Standing: 8/10

Resident Evil

A survival horror series based around a shady organization known as Umbrella, zombies and odd mutants included. Players fight zombies and various other mutants with limited resources. In 2019, the series made a very strong comeback with remakes of the old games, even staying true to their roots.
VidJio Current Standing: 9/10

Elder Scrolls

A role playing epic that takes place in a fantasy world known as Nirn on the continent of Tamriel. There is currently a total of 5 main titles, with a few spin offs. The last title was released in 2011 and is still modded and played to this day. Excellent replayability, but few and far between when it comes to releases.
VidJio Current Standing: 7/10

God of War

A high octane action game that takes place in ancient greece. You play as Kratos who has been used and abused by the gods. These stories follow Kratos on his path to vengence. Each game in the series is always strong. The latest Playstation for exclusive is no exception and was one of the strongest action games in recent memory.
VidJio Current Standing: 9/10

Doom

A popular shooter series that has had a recent comeback since originally launching decades ago. You play as a super marine who's sole job is to kill demons from hell. Due to the recent emergence, it has taken off and become a very popular series again.
VidJio Current Standing: 8/10

Diablo

An action role playing looting game. Choose your class and go up against the lords of hell, all while gaining precious randomized loot. Great games, although there have only been 3 releases since the first game launched over 20 years ago. The third installment, while still good, was nowhere near as fleshed out as its predecessor.
VidJio Current Standing: 7/10
